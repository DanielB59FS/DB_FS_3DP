enum GW::AUDIO::GATTENUATION

Listing of types of distance model attenuation curves a GSound3D or GMusic3D can use.

Enumerator
LINEAR 

This attenuation model is a 1/1 reduction in volume over distance.

Good for general looping ambience and low-detail background sounds that do not need tight 3d falloff settings. Also good for crossfading large radius ambient sounds.
